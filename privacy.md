# Privacy Policy – Codex Biblia

Last updated: 2026

Codex Biblia respects user privacy and is designed to function without collecting personal data.

## Data Collection

Codex Biblia does **not collect, store, or transmit personal information**.

The app does not require:

- account creation
- email registration
- personal identification
- location access

## Analytics

Codex Biblia does not use third-party analytics services.

## Internet Connection

The application works primarily offline.  
All quiz content and study material are stored locally within the app.

## In-App Purchases

Codex Biblia includes an optional **Tip Jar** feature that allows users to support the development of the application.

This is completely voluntary and does not unlock additional features.

All transactions are handled securely by **Apple's App Store infrastructure**.  
The app itself does not process or store payment information.

## Children’s Privacy

Codex Biblia does not knowingly collect data from children or users of any age.

## Changes to This Policy

If the privacy policy changes in future versions, the updated version will be published here.

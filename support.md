# Codex Biblia – Support

Thank you for using **Codex Biblia**.

Codex Biblia is a study companion designed to help users explore and learn the Bible through structured questions, book introductions, and references to Scripture.

## Frequently Asked Questions

### How does the quiz work?
Each quiz question is based on the content of the Bible. After answering, the app displays the correct answer and the related verse reference.

### Which Bible translations are used?
Codex Biblia includes references from public domain Bible translations:

- Daničić–Karadžić (Public Domain)
- King James Version – KJV (Public Domain)
- Luther Bible 1912 (Public Domain)

### Does the app require an internet connection?
No.  
The app works fully offline because all questions and content are stored locally in JSON format.

### Does the app collect personal data?
No. Codex Biblia does not collect personal user data.

### What is the Support / Tip Jar option?
The optional support option allows users to contribute to further development of the app.  
It does not unlock additional features.
